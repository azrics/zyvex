About SIA Zyvex

SIA Zyvex is a trading company based in Latvia, working in import, export, sourcing, and supply coordination for selected food and consumer product categories. In addition to international sourcing, we also identify suitable products from local markets in Latvia and support their export to Nordic countries.

Our Mission

To create reliable trade connections between suppliers and markets by delivering practical sourcing, supply, and distribution support with professionalism and consistency.

Our Vision

To grow SIA Zyvex into a trusted international trading partner known for quality, reliability, and long-term business value.

Our goal is to build practical and dependable trade relationships with suppliers, wholesalers, distributors, and retail partners. We believe in clear communication, steady execution, and business built on trust.

We are focused on identifying the right products, coordinating supply efficiently, and supporting growth opportunities across European markets, with a special interest in connecting Latvia-based supply opportunities to Nordic demand.

Our Services

Smart Logistics Within Europe and on the India-Europe Trade Route

SIA Zyvex offers reliable logistics solutions for businesses moving goods within Europe and between India and Europe. From freight coordination and customs support to warehousing and final delivery, we help businesses manage cargo with clarity, speed, and confidence.

Freight Coordination

Managing sea freight, air freight, and road transport across key trade routes.

Customs Support

Assistance with cross-border clearance and essential shipment documentation.

Warehousing

Safe storage, handling, and supply consolidation tailored to your operations.

Destination Logistics

Reliable final delivery and supply chain management within European borders.

What We Do

We act as your logistics partner for cross-border and regional cargo movement, coordinating sea freight, air freight, road transport, and storage through a trusted network of partners. Our goal is to make trade and transport simpler, faster, and more reliable.

Who We Serve

We work with Indian manufacturers, European importers, trading companies, wholesalers, and businesses looking for smooth logistics solutions within Europe and on the India-Europe trade route.

Our Approach

Rather than offering only transport, we provide complete logistics coordination with one clear point of contact from origin to destination.
